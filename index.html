Natarajan Chidambaram

R&T Scientist at Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology

About

Natarajan is a researcher in the field of AI for Software Engineering. Currently, he is working as an R&T Scientist at the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology. He obtained his Doctorate in Science (PhD) from the Department of Computer Science at University of Mons (UMONS), Belgium, and received his Master's degree in Data Science in Engineering from the Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands. He is also a member in the TRusted AI Labs (TRAIL), Belgium.

During his PhD, his research was focussed on improving bot identification in GitHub, detecting them based on their activity sequences and enhancing software engineering practices. He relied on AI techniques to develop various models that detect bots in GitHub, which inculuded an open-source CLI tool that researchers and practitioners can use to detect bots in GitHub at scale. In TRAIL, his research lied in Active Learning, semi supervised learning and generating synthetic images using Stable Diffusion with ControlNet. His other interests span over low-code software development, Generative AI, Data analysis, Data science and MLOps.
