About Me

Natarajan is a researcher in the field of AI for Software Engineering. He obtained his Doctorate in Science (PhD) from the Department of Computer Science at University of Mons (UMONS), Belgium. He is also a member in DigitalWallonia4.ai and TRAIL Factory.

Natarajan's research is focussed on detecting development bots in GitHub based on their activity sequences and enhancing software engineering practices. He predominantly depends on AI techniques to develop a model that detects bots accounts in GitHub and developed a CLI tool that researchers and practitioners can use it at scale. In TRAIL, his research lies in Active Learning, semi supervised learning and generating synthetic images using Stable Diffusion with ControlNet. His other interests span over Reinforcement Learning, Data analysis, Data science and MLOps. Prior to the current position, he received his Master's degree in Data Science in Engineering from the Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands.
